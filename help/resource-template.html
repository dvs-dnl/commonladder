Field reference

Field Required? Notes
Organization name Yes Official name of the organization or program
Tags Yes (1–4) Short labels describing service type (e.g., Emergency Shelter, SNAP, Legal Aid)
Description Yes One sentence in plain language — what they do and who they serve
Eligibility Yes Who qualifies; be specific about age, family status, geography, or other requirements
Hours Yes Current operating hours; note if drop-in or appointment only
Address Yes* *Use "Confidential — call for address" for DV shelters and safety programs
Phone Yes Use href="tel:[digits only]" for tap-to-call
Languages Recommended Languages spoken by staff or interpreter availability
Accessibility Recommended Wheelchair access, service animals, sensory accommodations
Cost Yes "Free" for all listings unless fees apply — note any sliding scale
Verified date Yes Month and year the listing was last verified by Common Ladder team
