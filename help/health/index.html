Health

Health resources near you

Free and low-cost medical care, dental, vision, prescriptions, and help enrolling in AHCCCS — you don't need insurance to be seen.

How to use this directory

Many health programs listed here serve patients without insurance or with AHCCCS. Bring any ID you have, but most sites will see you without one. If you need a prescription filled at no cost, ask about patient assistance programs. Call 211 to find the nearest clinic open today.
