Safety

Safety resources near you

Domestic violence shelters, crisis lines, and safety planning — confidential help available 24 hours a day.

How to use this directory

Shelter addresses for domestic violence programs are kept confidential to protect residents. Call the crisis line listed to be connected to intake. You do not need to have been physically harmed to ask for help — threats, coercive control, and emotional abuse qualify. All services on this page are free and confidential.
