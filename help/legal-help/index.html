Legal Help

Legal Help resources near you

Free legal aid for eviction defense, record clearing, benefits appeals, and other civil matters — you don't need money to get legal help.

How to use this directory

Free legal services can represent you in eviction court, help clear old criminal records, assist with benefit denials, and handle a range of civil matters. Most programs have an intake process — call or walk in to see if you qualify. Income limits typically apply but are set at a level that includes most people experiencing homelessness.
