Getting Around

Getting Around resources near you

Free or reduced-fare bus passes, ride programs, and transportation assistance to get to appointments, jobs, and services.

How to use this directory

Getting to appointments is one of the most common barriers people face when accessing services. Many programs listed here can provide free bus passes or arrange rides for medical appointments. If you have an AHCCCS health plan, call your plan's member services number to ask about non-emergency medical transportation.
