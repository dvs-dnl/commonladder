Family & Children

Family & Children resources near you

Family shelter, childcare, school enrollment help, and services for parents and kids — programs designed for families in crisis.

How to use this directory

Families with children have access to specialized programs that can help with shelter, childcare, school enrollment (McKinney-Vento protections apply), and case management. Most family programs keep parents and children together. If your family has been separated, call 211 for guidance.
