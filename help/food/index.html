Food

Food resources near you

Free meals, food banks, pantries, and help signing up for SNAP benefits — no referral or ID required at most sites.

How to use this directory

Most food programs do not require ID or proof of income — show up and ask for help. Food banks typically distribute groceries; meal sites serve hot food on a schedule. SNAP (food stamps) enrollment assistance is available through several nonprofits listed below. Use the ZIP search to find what's closest to you.
