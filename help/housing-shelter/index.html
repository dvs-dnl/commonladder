Housing & Shelter

Housing & Shelter resources near you

Emergency shelter, transitional housing, rapid rehousing, and permanent supportive housing — find a safe place tonight and a path toward stable housing.

How to use this directory

Browse the sample listings below to understand what each program offers. Use the ZIP code search to find programs serving your area. Each listing shows eligibility, hours, address, and a direct phone number — no account or sign-in required. If you need a bed tonight and aren't sure where to start, call 211 and tell them your situation.

Try this first

Not sure you need shelter? Try diversion first.

If you're at risk of losing housing but not yet on the street, diversion support may keep you housed — faster and with fewer barriers than shelter intake.
