Work & Training

Work & Training resources near you

Job placement, resume help, vocational training, and workforce programs designed for people facing barriers to employment.

How to use this directory

Workforce programs listed here serve people experiencing homelessness, long-term unemployment, or other employment barriers. Many can help you get a work permit, clothing for interviews, and certifications at no cost. Bring any ID or work documents you have — staff will work around gaps.
