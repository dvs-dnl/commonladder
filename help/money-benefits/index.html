Money & Benefits

Money & Benefits resources near you

Help applying for SSI, SNAP, TANF, utility assistance, and other government benefits — most programs have staff who will walk you through the paperwork.

How to use this directory

You don't have to navigate the benefits system alone. Nonprofits and government agencies listed here can help you apply for SNAP (food stamps), cash assistance, utility help, and disability benefits. Bring whatever ID and income documents you have — staff will work with what you have.
