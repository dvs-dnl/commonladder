How Sacramento's system works

Sacramento Steps Forward is the lead agency for the Sacramento City & County Continuum of Care (HUD code CA-503). Most shelter and housing intake runs through the Coordinated Access System, which you reach by dialing 2-1-1 (or (916) 498-1000). A 2-1-1 operator screens your situation and connects you to available help — you do not need an address or ID to call. A few programs take direct walk-ins, like Union Gospel Mission's men's shelter (nightly bed sign-up at 5:30 p.m.).
