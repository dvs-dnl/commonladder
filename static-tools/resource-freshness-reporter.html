Resource Freshness Reporter

The verification work queue for Common Ladder. It tracks every full-fat resource page — the ones that still publish specific provider names and phone numbers — and shows how long it's been since each was last verified. For the Mon/Wed verification engine and for case managers who want to know how current a page is before they rely on it.

How freshness is scored: Fresh = verified within 60 days · Due soon = 60–90 days · Overdue = more than 90 days. Days are counted live from each page's footer verification stamp. The 88 non-AZ navigators are honest triage stubs (no per-provider listings) and are intentionally not tracked here — there's nothing to verify on them.
