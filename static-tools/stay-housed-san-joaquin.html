Stay-Housed Navigator

A free, plain-language guide to rent help, utility assistance, and eviction prevention programs in San Joaquin County. We match your situation to the right local programs and tell you exactly how to apply — we don't submit anything on your behalf.

Programs ranked by how well they match what you told us. Tap each one to see exactly how to apply, what they'll ask, and what to bring.

About these programs: Most pay your landlord or utility company directly, not you. They're nonprofits and county agencies — they can run out of funds, so applying to two or three at once is a normal strategy, not a problem.

Safety first.

If you're in an unsafe situation, these programs are confidential — they don't share your address with anyone outside their organization. You can call them and ask without committing to anything.

If you're in danger right now, call 911 or the National DV Hotline: 1-800-799-7233.
