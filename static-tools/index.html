Find your Continuum of Care

Every county in the US is covered by a HUD-funded Continuum of Care (CoC) that coordinates homeless response. Even if Common Ladder doesn't have a navigator yet, your CoC is the right next step.

How navigators work

Three things every navigator does.

🗺️

Maps the whole system

Every shelter, food program, health clinic, legal aid office, and benefits navigator in one place — categorized and searchable.

✓

Verified contacts

Phone numbers, addresses, hours, eligibility, and access requirements — verified at intake and re-checked quarterly.

🖨️

Printable handouts

Every page prints cleanly so case managers can hand a paper copy to a client without internet access.
