Do you qualify for SNAP food benefits?

Four quick questions. About 1 minute. No account required.

Answer what you can — nothing is required, and nothing you type leaves your device.

Your answers stay on your device No signup required Free to use

This is a pre-screening tool, not an application or an eligibility decision — only Arizona DES can decide. Dollar figures are the federal FY 2026 limits (in effect Oct 1, 2025–Sep 30, 2026) and change every year. When in doubt, applying is free — apply anyway or call 211.

Do you live in Arizona?

SNAP is a federal program, but each state sets some of its own rules and income limits. This tool uses Arizona's limits. If you're elsewhere, we'll point you to your state.

How many people are in your household?

Count everyone who lives with you and buys and cooks food together — including yourself and any children. Roommates who buy their own food don't count.

Does anyone in your household…

Select all that apply. Any of these can help you qualify — or skip the income test entirely. If none apply, choose "None of these."

Roughly, how much does your household earn before taxes each month?

Add up everyone's income before anything is taken out: wages, self-employment, unemployment, child support, Social Security, disability. A rough number is fine. If you have no income right now, enter 0.
