Flagstaff VA Clinic — Homeless Care Coordinator

🌱 Youth Homeless Services (24 & under)
Flagstaff / Coconino County
Research
Flagstaff Shelter Services — Emergency Shelter & CE Access
4185 E Huntington Dr, Flagstaff, AZ 86004
(928) 225-2533 — open 24/7, 365 days a year
Walk in any time. Serves all adults including youth. Front Door CE assessments done on-site (Mon–Thu 8am–4pm or by appointment).
Homeless Youth Connection (HYC)
Serves youth ages 13–21. Provides housing, basic needs, case management, and education support through the Empowering Youth for the Future (EYFTF) program. Active in Flagstaff schools and community. Contact Flagstaff Shelter Services to be connected.
Front Door — Coordinated Entry
Walk in to Flagstaff Shelter Services or Catholic Charities (2101 N 4th St).
Email: frontdoor@flagshelter.org
Confidence: Research — sourced from agency websites. A dedicated youth-specific drop-in center was not confirmed as of June 2026; verify current HYC intake process locally. Last checked: June 2026.

🏠 Permanent Supportive Housing
Chronically Homeless — Flagstaff / Coconino County
Research
Front Door — Coordinated Entry Assessment (Required First Step)
PSH in Coconino County is accessed through the Front Door CE system. You must be assessed to get on the PSH referral list.

Access point: Flagstaff Shelter Services, 4185 E Huntington Dr
(928) 225-2533 — walk in Mon–Thu 8am–4pm, or call ahead
Email: frontdoor@flagshelter.org
Catholic Charities — Permanent Supportive Housing
Operates approximately 50 PSH units in Coconino County for people with long-term homelessness and a disabling condition. Residents pay no more than 30% of monthly income in rent. Access through Front Door CE assessment.
Contact: (928) 774-9125
Emergency Shelter (While Waiting)
Flagstaff Shelter Services is open 24/7 while you wait for PSH placement. Case managers on-site can help navigate the process.
Confidence: Research — PSH unit count (~50) sourced from citizenportal.ai reporting on Catholic Charities. Actual availability varies. Last checked: June 2026.
🚪 Emergency Shelter — Single Adults
Flagstaff / Coconino County
Research
Flagstaff Shelter Services — Emergency Shelter
4185 E Huntington Dr, Flagstaff, AZ 86004
(928) 225-2533 ext. 307
Open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Walk in — no appointment needed for shelter.
Services include meals, clothing, hygiene supplies, showers, mailing address, case management, and referrals. Serves all adults in crisis.

Note: High demand — overflow shelter in operation as of 2025. A new expanded facility was under construction to nearly double capacity.
Front Door — Coordinated Entry (CE) Assessment
Get assessed for rapid re-housing, transitional housing, and permanent supportive housing.
Walk in to Flagstaff Shelter Services or Catholic Charities (2101 N 4th St, (928) 774-9125).
Assessments: Mon–Thu 8am–4pm. Email: frontdoor@flagshelter.org
211 Arizona
Call or text 211 for additional resources, food, and community services.
