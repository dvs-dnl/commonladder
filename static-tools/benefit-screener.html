Are you a US citizen or legal permanent resident (green card holder)?

Most federal benefits require citizenship or a qualifying immigration status. If you're not sure, answer "Not sure" — you may still qualify for some programs.

Do you have children in your household?

This helps us find programs like SNAP, WIC, TANF, and tax credits that depend on family size and structure.

Do any of these apply to you?

Select all that apply. "Long-term" means lasting or expected to last at least 12 months.

About how much money does your household bring in each month?

Include all income: wages, SSI/SSDI, child support, disability payments, anything. If you're not sure or have no income right now, select $0.

Do you currently have health insurance?

This includes Medicaid (AHCCCS in Arizona), Medicare, employer coverage, or any other plan.

What best describes your housing situation right now?

No wrong answer. This helps us surface the most urgent programs.

What's your ZIP code?

We use this to surface local emergency contacts and resources in your area. This is optional — skip it if you'd prefer.
